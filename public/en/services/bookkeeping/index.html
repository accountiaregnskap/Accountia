Common questions about bookkeeping

Short and clear information before you decide.

Who is this service for?
The service fits both small and medium-sized businesses that want reliable bookkeeping, solid overview, and less administration.
Can you take over an existing bookkeeping setup?
Yes. We plan the transition, review balances, and establish new routines without disrupting daily operations.
Do I need to change accounting system?
Not necessarily. We can work in your current system, or help you choose a setup that fits better.
How do I keep overview of the numbers?
You receive regular reporting and clear summaries, and we explain the key points in plain language.
