Common questions about bookkeeping

Short and clear information before you decide.

Who is this service for?
The service fits both small and medium-sized businesses that want reliable bookkeeping, better overview, and less internal admin.
Can you take over an existing setup?
Yes. We plan the transition, review balances, and establish new routines without disturbing daily operations.

Not necessarily. We can work in your current tools, or help you choose a setup that fits better.
How do I keep overview of the numbers?
