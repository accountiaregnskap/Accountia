How we prepare a quote

A good quote is not just about one number. It starts with understanding how your business works and what kind of support you actually need.

When we prepare a quote, we typically look at:

  • how the accounting work is handled today
  • which systems and routines you already use
  • how much ongoing follow-up you want
  • whether you need support with payroll, VAT, reporting or advisory
  • how we can create a setup that works well over time

The goal is to give you a clear proposal that fits your business, rather than forcing you into a standard package that does not match reality.

What you can expect from us

We value clear communication from the first conversation. You should understand how we think, what we recommend and how we would structure the collaboration.

We also want the path to onboarding to feel simple. That is why we guide you through the process, answer questions along the way and make sure the transition feels predictable.
